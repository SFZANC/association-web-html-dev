About Our Association

The San Felipe Zambales Association of Northern California (SFZANC) was founded in 1974. It was incorporated on March 27, 2006, and officially became a non-profit organization in 2014. The organization's file number is 2860942.

The registered agent on file for the organization is Ruth F. Hernandez. The association is located at:

P.O. Box 61795, Sunnyvale, CA 94088
Phone: 408-476-6711

Our Mission

In oneness and solidarity, the organization, SFZANC ensures the financial assistance to the less fortunate children in our hometown, San Felipe, Zambales, Philippines.
